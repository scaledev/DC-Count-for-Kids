Mobile, Mobile Everywhere. And Yet...

Mobile-phone subscriptions now outnumber the fixed-line variety by nearly two-to-one in the United States; nine in 10 Americans have a mobile. And globally speaking, that's nothing -- a handful of countries are nearing two mobiles per resident, and in many landline-deprived African nations, the mobile-to-fixed ratio tops 200:1.
